About the System

The Citizen Grievance Management System is designed to provide citizens with a transparent and efficient platform for raising, tracking, and resolving grievances related to society and community services. Our mission is to bridge the gap between citizens and the administration through technology.

2

Features Available

  • Register and manage citizen accounts
  • Raise complaints and grievances
  • Track complaint status in real time
  • View complete complaint history
  • Update profile information
  • Change account password securely
  • Receive updates on complaint progress
  • Get notified on officer assignments
